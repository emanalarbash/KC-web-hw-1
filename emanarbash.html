The UNO GAME

Each player draws a card. Player with the highest point value is the dealer. Shuffle the deck. Each player is dealt 7 cards. Place the remaining cards facedown to form a draw pile. Turn over the top card of the draw pile to begin a discard pile. If the top card is a Wild or Wild Draw 4, return it to the deck and pick another card. For all other cards, see directions that follow.
